What is CardScope?

CardScope helps you maximize credit card rewards by instantly recommending the best card to use at any store or merchant. Simply enter a store name (or use location detection) and get personalized suggestions based on your actual credit cards.

🌟 Key Features

  • Add Your Cards: Store all your credit cards in one place
  • Smart Suggestions: Get instant recommendations for any store
  • Location Detection: Automatically detect nearby stores
  • Category Tracking: Understand rewards by category (groceries, gas, dining, etc.)
  • Quarterly Alerts: Track reward categories that change each quarter
